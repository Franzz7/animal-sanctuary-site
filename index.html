About Our Sanctuary

At Thors Field Sanctuary, we provide lifelong care for rescued animals who have come from neglect, abandonment, or unsafe environments.

Our mission is simple: to offer every animal a peaceful home, proper veterinary care, nutritious food, and kindness every single day.

Every donation helps us feed, shelter, and protect animals who now have a second chance at life.

What your support helps fund

  • Feed and bedding
  • Veterinary care
  • Stable and shelter maintenance
  • Daily enrichment and rehabilitation
  • Emergency rescue support
