Life at Thors Field Sanctuary

Thors Field Animal Sanctuary is a safe home for animals who have been neglected, abandoned, or simply left without the care they deserve. Here, they are given time, safety, proper food, medical support, and the simple kindness that every animal should know.

Life at the sanctuary is about more than rescue. It is about creating peace, routine, and comfort. It is muddy noses, warm bedding, quiet grooming, full food bowls, and animals slowly learning that they are safe.

Every donation and every sponsor helps make that possible.

What your support helps provide

  • Daily feed, hay, and bedding
  • Veterinary treatment and check-ups
  • Safe shelters, stables, and enclosures
  • Rehabilitation, enrichment, and care
  • Rescue support for animals in urgent need

The Animals Make This Place Special
