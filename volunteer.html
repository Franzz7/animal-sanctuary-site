Get Involved

Volunteer With Us

Join us in giving rescued animals the care, safety, and compassion they deserve. Whether you can help regularly or occasionally, your time can make a real difference.

Why Volunteer

Be Part of Something Meaningful

Volunteers are a huge part of life at Thors Field Animal Sanctuary. From helping with feeding and cleaning to spending time with the animals and assisting with day-to-day jobs, every bit of support matters.

Volunteering is a chance to be part of something meaningful, connect with animals, and help create a safe and loving environment for residents who have been rescued from difficult situations.

We welcome people who are kind, reliable, hard-working, and willing to learn. Experience can be helpful, but it is not always necessary.

🤝

Ways you may be able to help

  • Feeding and refreshing water
  • Cleaning shelters, stables, and enclosures
  • General maintenance and tidying
  • Grooming and spending time with animals
  • Helping during events and fundraising days
  • Supporting transport or supply runs
Who We're Looking For

Kindness Over Experience

We're looking for caring people who genuinely want to help. You do not need to be perfect or have years of experience — what matters most is patience, kindness, consistency, and respect for the animals and the sanctuary.
